FAQ

Here are the answers to your questions

Find quick answers to the most common support questions.

What is BuildFlow?
BuildFlow is an all-in-one construction management platform that unifies Owners, Supervisors, Workers, and Lenders. It tracks everything from ground break to final handover in real-time.
How secure is my data on BuildFlow?
We use industry-standard bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all data. Your project blueprints, financial documents, and team communications are protected by multiple layers of security.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. You can scale your plan up or down as your firm grows. Changes take effect immediately, and annual plans come with a 20% discount.
What file types does BuildFlow support?
BuildFlow supports all major construction file formats, including DWG, PDF, high-res site photos, 4K video submissions, and Excel-based financial logs.
Is there a limit on file uploads?
Starter plans include 50GB of storage. Pro plans feature 500GB, and Enterprise plans offer unlimited storage for large-scale portfolios and multi-year projects.
